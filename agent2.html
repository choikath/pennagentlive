Penn Dash

Clinical data are often presented in the way they were collected (e.g. vital signs, flowsheets, laboratory, radiology) rather than the way they should be interpreted. The classic vertical data presentation of EMRs makes it naturally hard for clinicians to synthesize data across modalities to make critical medical decisions.

We are transforming clinical information by designing dashboards that integrate data by system (cardiovascular, infectious disease, endocrine, etc). This horizontal integration of data means fewer clicks, less work, and a deeper understanding of data than ever before.

Designed by David Do MD & Eugene Gitelman MD, and Ian Oppenheim MD

Agent


Led product from 4 alpha testers to 55+ beta testers across 9 care roles and 8 disciplines in first two months. Gained support from 10+ clinical partners. Ran four validation pilots with the medicine and intensive care units at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania.

Agent delivers smart alerts to providers on their mobile devices, to give them a more timely
awareness of patient data when they are away from the desktop electronic medical record.
The webapp platform allows customized, "opt-in" setup by users.


Dash

Team developed webapp to transform delivery of clinical information according to workflow. Co-designed novel customized data interfaces with clinical directors of infectious disease, endocrinology, and intensive care services.

Dash transforms clinical information by integrating data by system (e.g. cardiovascular,
infectious disease, endocrine), rather than the current EMR data presentation according
to the way data is collected (e.g. vital signs, flowsheets, laboratory, radiology).
